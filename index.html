Bringing Seaweed Revolution in India

Brahma Kamal Research LLP is commercializing the vast potential of seaweed and seaweed products. Brahma Kamal aims to create a sustainable ecosystem that will leverage local fisherman communities to cultivate, harvest, and trade seaweed by taking it as a livelihood option. The company focuses on large-scale cultivation and value chain development of important seaweed species for their commercial use in agriculture as bio stimulants, FMCG, Food, and dairy industries as ingredients, besides creating a sustainable ecosystem that can help us fight climate change.

Key Activities

SEAWEED CULTIVATION

Seaweed cultivation holds immense potential for India's coastal regions, offering sustainable economic and environmental benefits. Brahmakamal is creating low cost-high impact mechanized techniques to cultivate seaweed in open sea using traditional boats existing with fisherman.

LIVELIHOOD GENERATION

Seaweed cultivation provides an alternative source of income to the fishermen community who often face income loss due to adverse weather conditions and overfishing. Seaweed reduces vulnerability and ensures financial stability, improving the livelihoods of the coastal communities. Brahma Kamal works closely with the coastal community to train them on seaweed cultivation & processing.

PRODUCTION OF PLANTBIO STIMULANTS

Seaweed-based bio stimulants are agricultural inputs that mitigate abiotic stress like heat and drought and enhance plant productivity through increased biological activity. Bio stimulants in the long run can revitalize the soil & hence reduce farmerâ€™s dependence on fertilizers. Brahma kamal is formulating bio stimulants for different seaweed species for usage in agriculture

Seaweed Opportunity

In India

Seaweed, are emerging as a significant natural resource in India, attracting attention for their diverse applications and economic potential. Seaweed has proven to be a versatile marine resource, offering a multitude of benefits in various sectors.

SCOPE FOR EXPONENTIAL GROWTH

India produced about 34000 Tonnes of seaweed which was only about 0.01% of global seaweed production which stood at around 35 Million Tonnes.

IMPORT SUBSTITUTION

India is currently a net importer of seaweed products due to the lack of cultivation of both quantity & quality of seaweeds on the Indian coast

GOVERNMENT SUPPORT

The government of India under PMMSY has allocated Rs 640 Crores to increase seaweed production and employ coastal fisherwomen. India has set a target of 9.7 million tons seaweed production by 2025

USAGE IN DIVERSE APPLICATIONS

Seaweed has its usage in diverse fields like fertilizer, animal feed, food, bioplastic, biofuel, nutraceuticals, and pharma industries thus helping growers of seaweed get easy market for their produce

Benefits Of Seaweed

Biodiversity

Seaweed cultivation offers significant benefits to the biodiversity of ocean ecosystems. As a natural habitat for various marine species, seaweed provides shelter, food, and breeding grounds, fostering a diverse and resilient ecosystem.... Additionally, seaweed absorbs excess nutrients, and sequesters carbon thus mitigating water pollution, helping fight climate change and supporting the overall health and balance of marine environments

Economic Value

Seaweed cultivation presents a promising economic avenue for India. Rich in vitamins, minerals, and compounds with commercial value, seaweed serves as a valuable resource for various industries, including food, pharmaceuticals, ... and cosmetics. Its sustainable cultivation can generate income, provide employment opportunities, and contribute to the country's economic growth.
